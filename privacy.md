# DeScroll Privacy Policy

   1. Data Privacy Commitment
   
	DeScroll was built on the principle of privacy. We believe
    your reading habits and interests should remain your own.
   
   2. Local-Only Processing

	The core functionality of DeScroll—bookmark analysis and feed
    discovery—happens entirely within your browser. We do not have a 
    central server that stores your personal data.
   
   3. Analytics & Telemetry

	To help improve the extension, we collect anonymous usage data
    via Google Analytics 4 (GA4) using the Measurement Protocol. This
    data is transmitted to Google's servers.

    We use an anonymous, locally-generated client ID to distinguish
    unique installations without identifying individuals. We track 
    limited events, such as when the onboarding process starts and
    when feed discovery completes.

    You can opt out of this telemetry at any time through the 
    extension's settings page.

    We do NOT track the specific URLs in your bookmarks or the content
    of the feeds you read.

   4. Permissions Disclosure

	• Bookmarks: Used to identify the sources you want to follow.
	• Network Access: Used only to fetch public RSS/Atom feeds from your bookmarked sites and to send anonymous telemetry.
	• Storage: Used to persist your theme choices, "seen" history, and anonymous client ID locally.
   
   5. Transparency

	DeScroll is open-source. While we use analytics to understand 
    project usage and growth, we strive to keep data collection 
    minimal and anonymous. Your attention is the only thing we want 
    to help you protect.
