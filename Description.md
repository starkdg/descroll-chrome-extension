#	DeScroll: Reclaim Your Feed
  
   Most news feeds are designed to keep you scrolling. DeScroll is
   designed to keep you informed. It replaces your default New Tab
   page with a high-quality, infinite feed sourced entirely from the
   websites you actually care about.
   
##   How it works:

	DeScroll transforms your browser bookmarks into a personal newsroom.
	Simply bookmark your favorite blogs, news sites, or portfolios, and
	DeScroll’s discovery engine automatically finds the latest articles
	and updates for you.
   
##   Key Features:

	- Intentional Minimalism: A clean, distraction-free design that puts 
	  the focus entirely on the content.

	- Privacy-First Architecture: Your bookmarks and reading history 
	  never leave your device. DeScroll operates 100% locally.
  
	- Smart Discovery: No need to manage RSS feeds. Just bookmark a 
	  domain, and we’ll find the content.

	- Professional Theming: Sophisticated palettes like "Emerald Forest," 
	  "Midnight Gold," and "Ethereal Minimalist" to suit your style.
   
##   Why DeScroll?

	Break the "distraction loop" of algorithmic social media. Start every 
	new tab with intention and discover something meaningful from the 
	sources you trust.
