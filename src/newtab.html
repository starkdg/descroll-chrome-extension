Reclaim your news feed with content sourced from your own bookmarks.

To get started, tell us which bookmark folder contains your favorite sources:

Tip: You can use an existing bookmark folder or type a new name. If the folder doesn't exist, DeScroll will create it with starter bookmarks to get you going. You can change this later in the Settings.

DeScroll will look for RSS feeds and sitemaps on the domains you've bookmarked in this folder.

Note: On the next step, Chrome will ask for permission to "Read and change your data on all websites." DeScroll needs this to fetch RSS feeds from your bookmarks. Your data never leaves your device.
